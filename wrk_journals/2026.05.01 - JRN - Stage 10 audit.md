- **Empty `crates/rsiprtp-transport/` directory.** Captured in
  supervisor's journal as a known Windows-file-lock leftover deferred

  `crates/rsiprtp/Cargo.toml` (no `crossbeam.workspace`,

  into the lock graph. But the CHANGELOG line "Unused `crossbeam` and

  still *defined* in workspace, just no longer *consumed*. Trivial
  cleanup, non-blocking.
- **Single transient lib-test failure once across 6 runs** ‚Äî could
  not reproduce on 5 subsequent runs. The `--test-threads=1`
  invocation makes timing-flake unlikely; possibly a one-off
  Windows AV / fs jitter. Worth investigating before publish if it

  block line 328 should be cleaned up retroactively if the HLD is
