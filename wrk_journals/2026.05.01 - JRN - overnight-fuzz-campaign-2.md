### Sibling-overflow findings (deferred)

The triage agent surveyed adjacent `wrapping_sub(_) as i32` users in `jitter.rs` and reported two latent overflow risks:

1. `gap > self.target_delay as i32 * 2` — `target_delay` is config-clamped, but `* 2` is unchecked.
2. `samples_per_packet as i32` — config field is `u32`, so values `≥ 0x8000_0000` flip sign in the cast.

Both are config-controlled (not reachable from peer-supplied bytes), so left unmodified per the "no speculative fixes" rule. Worth tracking as a follow-up cleanup if the team wants belt-and-braces hardening.
